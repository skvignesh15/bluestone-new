About Us

We are engaged in providing Best solutions in event management & Stage Decoration.
Blue Stone Decors And Events is one of emerging Event Management Providers in Coimbatore that came into existence to provide the best for all Events and Wedding Planning needs in Coimbatore.

Our services includes Wedding Planning, Event management, Stage Decorations, Corporate Events, Exhibitions, Birthday Party, Theme Party, Private Party, etc.

Our Services

Event Management

Behind each and every successful event, one of the fundamental elements is excellent management. Our team of highly skilled professionals at Blue Stone Decors And Events Will Provide combined experience that you need for event management of all types and sizes. If you are planning conferences, gala dinners, AGM’s, seminars & meetings, product launches, special and public events or looking for other services like in-house event management services, incentives, corporate hospitality, official site openings and conference team building then Blue Stone Decors And Events can be your destination.

Stage Decoration

An elegant and beautifully created stage enhances the glamour of your special occasion and amazes your guests. We have a stock a variety of Wedding Stages, Wedding Backdrops, Top tables, Wedding Chairs, Wedding Sofas, Chaise, Throne Chairs and other accessories to personalise your stage to your style. Whether it’s a wedding, nikkah, walima, mehndi or a reception, our personalised service means that we can ensure the colour and theme of your wedding is carried out throughout the design. Completely glamorise your venue with elegant finishing touch by Blue Stone Decors And Events.
